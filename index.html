How Does it Work

If you request an Uber ride, when enrolled in this program, your total charge will be the next whole number.

Lets say your ride costed you a total of $18.28, but you will pay $19.00 (The next whole number).

A majority of the money will go towards providing free rides to the homeless people, and providing funding for shelters, kitchens, and health services.

5% of the money donated goes into a raffle for Uber riders in this program. View "Helpful links" for more information.
